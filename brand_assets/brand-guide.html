Signature Rules

1Clear space: minimum 1× the cap-height of "X" on every side. Treat it like the rim of a plate — never crowd the food.
2Minimum size: 88 px wide on screen, 24 mm in print. Below this, drop the arc text and use the wordmark only.
3Color: always Brand Blue on Paper Cream or Warm Amber. Never on bright white, never inverted in white on blue (sacrifices contrast warmth).
4Lockup integrity: the Chinese name 胡希瑞 sits above the romanization XIRUI HU. They are inseparable — never use one without the other.
5Don't: recolor, outline, rotate beyond ±2°, stretch, or set on a photograph without a paper-cream backplate.

The Jomhuria Rule

Jomhuria is a tall, condensed display face. Its cap-height is roughly 62% of its set font-size, so a font-size: 48px Jomhuria header looks like a 30px header in any other typeface. To stay on the type scale, always set Jomhuria at ~1.6× the optical step. Use the --jh-* tokens in the next section — they're pre-multiplied. Never eyeball it; consistency is the brand promise.

Grid & Layout

  • Base unit: 8 px. Every margin, padding, gap and component dimension is a multiple of this — no orphan pixel values.
  • Max page width: 1280 px with --s-16 (64 px) outer gutters on desktop, --s-6 (24 px) on mobile.
  • Columns: 12-column grid · --s-6 (24 px) gutters · adaptive to 6 cols at 768 px and 4 cols at 480 px.
  • Card radius: --r-card: 4 px for cards, --r-pill: 999 px for buttons. Two values, no exceptions.
  • Hairline: 1 px solid Ink Black at 100 % opacity — never use shadow as a divider.
  • Vertical rhythm: baseline-aligned to 8 px. Section spacing = --s-16 (64 px) min between blocks.

Voice & Detail

How the brand speaks

Always Do

  • Write like a host welcoming you to a small restaurant — warm, knowledgeable, never stiff.
  • Use food metaphors sparingly — they're a flavor, not the whole meal.
  • Pair every claim with the receipt: numbers, screenshots, prototypes.
  • Keep paragraphs to ≤ 3 sentences. White space is the second course.
  • Run every page through the type-scale and 8 px grid before shipping.

Never Do

  • Don't use stock photography or generic gradients — every image is a deliberate prop.
  • Don't introduce new colors, fonts, or spacing values without updating this guide.
  • Don't set Jomhuria below 32 px — it loses legibility and breaks the optical scale.
  • Don't write "passionate", "innovative", "leverage" — generic brand-speak.
  • Don't ship a page where any value isn't a documented token. Detail is the brand.
